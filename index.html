Introduction video for the project:

Movies are one of the most common form of entertainment in the world today, whether it is to sit down with a fun comedy movie or to get the adrenaline pumping with a horror movie. Since movies are so popular there are a big market for these which many production companies want to be a part of, which results in many new movies being released every single year. The increasing amount of movies each year results in more amazing movies to chose from, but sadly also alot of horrible movies which is not worth watching. Watching horrible movies is a waste of time which is why this project focuses on predicting if a movie is worth watching, based on actors in the movie and previous reviews of movies these actors has been in.
